ReefTracker Feature Overview

Last updated: 23 July 2026

ReefTracker is an offline-first reef-aquarium companion for Android and iOS. Log your water tests in seconds and see at a glance whether every value is healthy – with color-coded safe ranges, history graphs, trends and early warnings, dosing tools, maintenance reminders, and safe backups. Everything is stored on your device: no account, no cloud requirement, no ads.

Each feature below is either included for everyone (Standard) or part of the ReefTracker Pro tier (PRO). Features marked EXPERIMENTAL stay hidden until you switch them on in Settings → Experimental.

Tank stability score – the second axis of tank health: how much values have been swinging over the last 30/60/90 days, independent of where they sit nowPRO

FeatureEdition

Optional quick-scan camera button next to "Add reading" – one-tap scanning for checker owners, no lost screen space for everyone else (off by default)Standard
