Know your reef inside out – your whole routine in one app

Built by a reef keeper, for reef keepers – from the daily alkalinity test to the yearly membrane change.

🧪Water parameter tracking

All core reef parameters with color-coded safe ranges you can tune per tank. Batch entry, test sets for your routines, and typo protection so a slipped decimal never wrecks your data.

💚Tank health at a glance

A weighted 0–100 health score with a per-parameter breakdown, plus a stability score and prioritized plain-language insights PRO – computed entirely on your device.

📈Graphs, trends & early warnings

Zoomable history graphs with your safe range shaded behind the data, trend detection, and forecasts that warn you before a drifting value leaves its range.

💧Dosing tools

A supplement plan built on a catalog of reef brands, full dosing history, and calculators PRO that turn your readings into a recommended daily dose or a safe correction plan.

🔔Maintenance & reminders

Water changes, media changes and cleanings in one log, an elastic maintenance schedule that never stacks phantom overdue warnings, RO/DI filter life tracking, and opt-in notifications.

🔬Microelements & ICP

The full 33-element trace panel with seawater-anchored ranges, custom element views, and one-step import of Fauna Marin and ATI ICP reports PRO.

📲Hanna checker integration

Import your Hanna Lab test history, run measurements live over Bluetooth, or scan a pocket checker's display with your camera – no typing PRO.

🛟Safe backups

One-file backups shared anywhere, automatic local backups, optional Google Drive sync PRO, CSV export, and an "Ask your AI" summary you can paste into any AI chat.

🌍In your language, your units

Fully localized in English, German, French, Italian, Polish, Czech and Russian – with more languages on the way. °C or °F, ppt or SG, liters or gallons, light or dark theme – your choice.

Your data stays yours

ReefTracker is 100 % offline. No account, no sign-up, no tracking, no ads. Your readings live on your device and leave it only when you export or back them up. Read the privacy policy – it's short.
